About Fat duck auto detailing company

Fat Duck Auto Detailing is your go-to choice for comprehensive auto detailing services in Maynard Rc. As a locally-owned business, we take pride in our deep-rooted connection to the community and our genuine passion for cars. Our dedicated team understands the unique needs of each vehicle, ensuring that every detail is handled with the utmost care and precision.

We’re more than just a car wash—we’re detailers who care about your vehicle as much as you do. We understand that your car is an important part of your life, and we go the extra mile to ensure it looks and performs its best. Whether it's enhancing its appearance or protecting its value, we strive to exceed your expectations every time.
